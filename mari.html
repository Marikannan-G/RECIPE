Indian Cuisine

  • Butter Chicken (North India)
    Description: A creamy tomato-based curry with tender pieces of chicken.
    Ingredients: Chicken, Tomatoes, Butter, Cream, Onions, Ginger-garlic paste, Spices (garam masala, chili powder, turmeric)
    Recipe: 1. Marinate chicken. 2. Sauté onions and ginger-garlic paste. 3. Add tomatoes and spices. 4. Cook until tomatoes soften. 5. Add chicken and cook. 6. Stir in butter and cream. 7. Simmer and serve.
  • Biryani (Various)
    Description: A fragrant rice dish cooked with spices, meat, or vegetables.
    Ingredients: Rice, Meat (chicken, mutton, or vegetables), Onions, Ginger-garlic paste, Yogurt, Spices (biryani masala, saffron), Mint leaves, Coriander leaves
    Recipe: 1. Marinate meat/vegetables. 2. Sauté onions and ginger-garlic paste. 3. Layer rice and meat/vegetables. 4. Cook on low heat. 5. Garnish with mint and coriander leaves.

Chinese Cuisine

Kung Pao Chicken (宫保鸡丁)

A spicy stir-fry dish with chicken, peanuts, vegetables, and chili peppers.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Peanuts
  • Dried chili peppers
  • Scallions
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce

Recipe:

  1. Cut chicken into cubes and marinate with soy sauce.
  2. Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry chicken until cooked.
  3. Add chili peppers, scallions, ginger, and garlic and stir-fry until fragrant.
  4. Add peanuts and stir-fry.
  5. Serve hot.

Mapo Tofu (麻婆豆腐)

A spicy and flavorful tofu dish with ground meat in a chili-bean sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Tofu
  • Ground pork or beef
  • Doubanjiang (chili bean paste)
  • Soy sauce

Recipe:

  1. Cut tofu into cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry ground meat until cooked.
  3. Add doubanjiang, garlic, and ginger and stir-fry until fragrant.
  4. Add soy sauce and water and bring to a boil.
  5. Add tofu and gently simmer.
  6. Serve hot.

Italian Cuisine

Pizza Margherita

Cuisine: Italian | Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Servings: 4

A classic Italian pizza with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Arrange mozzarella slices on top.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted.
  6. Top with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Cuisine: Italian | Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Servings: 4

A classic Roman pasta dish with eggs, pancetta, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 4 oz pancetta, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup Pecorino Romano cheese, grated

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. While pasta cooks, sauté pancetta in a skillet until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  5. Add pasta to skillet with pancetta.
  6. Remove skillet from heat, add egg mixture, and toss quickly.
  7. Add pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  8. Serve immediately.
